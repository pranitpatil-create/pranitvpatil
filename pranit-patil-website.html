01 — About

Academic Profile

I am Prof. Pranit V. Patil, Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU), Lonere, Maharashtra. With over 8 years of teaching experience and 1 year of industrial exposure, I bring both theoretical depth and practical insight to my students.

My academic journey began at Mumbai University (B.E., 2014 – First Class), followed by my postgraduate studies at the prestigious Government College of Engineering, Karad (M.Tech). I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. at DBATU, with a focus on advanced power systems analysis.

I have contributed to the academic community through 7 published international research papers. I teach both undergraduate and postgraduate (M.Tech) students, nurturing the next generation of electrical engineers.
